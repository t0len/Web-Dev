Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 200g
Milk 300ml
Eggs 2

Step 1: Mix flour, milk, and eggs into a smooth batter.

Step 2: Heat a non-stick pan and pour a ladle of batter.

Step 3: Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook the other side.

Step 4: Serve with syrup, butter, or your favorite toppings.
